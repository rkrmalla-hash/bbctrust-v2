About BBC Trust

Dedicated To Building A Better Society

Bapu Bhushanum Charitable Trust is a non-profit organization committed to creating lasting positive change through compassion, education, healthcare, social welfare and community development.

Empowering Communities Through Service

Our mission is to uplift underprivileged communities by providing opportunities for education, healthcare, food assistance and social support while promoting dignity, equality and hope.
